​New Regulations Will Help State Understand COVID-19 Impacts on California's Diverse Communities

To better understand the impact of COVID-19 on California’s diverse communities, the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) took regulatory action today, which goes into effect immediately, expanding data reporting requirements for providers and laboratories.
